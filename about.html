About Charlene

Hi! I'm Charlene Woods, a Toronto REALTOR®, master negotiator, and founder of Charlene In The City. I work with discerning buyers and sellers who expect more-more service, more strategy, and more sophistication - and I deliver exactly that. My clients aren't looking for average, and neither am I. With a background in business, coaching, and personal development, I bring more than market knowledge.

I bring presence, poise, and precision. I'm playful but polished, charming but commanding, and always two steps ahead of the deal. Whether you're listing a refined family home in Leaside or making power moves in Yorkville, I show up fully. My listings stand out because I don't just market homes - I craft an experience.

I partner with the city's best stagers, designers, photographers, and advisors to position your home to perform at the highest level. Every touchpoint matters. Clients work with me when they want a strategic advantage. They refer me when they want to raise the bar. And they stay connected because they know I genuinely care. You'll never feel like “just another deal.” With me, you're in trusted hands, with someone who sees the big picture, sweats the details, and shows up with unmatched commitment.

Book a discovery call - let's have a real conversation about your goals, your property, your dream future, and how I can help you get there. I'll bring the strategy, the insight, and the energy. You just bring your vision.
